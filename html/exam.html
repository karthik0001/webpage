How I cleared the AWS Certified Solutions Architect -Associate Exam

My preparation took two and a half weeks. Spent around 5-6 hours each day

  1. Most of my preparation was from acloud.guru lectures
    • I revisited few lectures at 1.3x speed
    • VPC lab is very important, this is the only lab I did thrice
    • I made rough notes while listening to the lectures, it helped to review before the exam
    • I scored 90+ in individual quiz. I scored low in the Final Practice Exam, it was very difficult. But it prepared me to manage time
  2. Read the FAQs of each service
      Focused on few of my weak topics after taking practice exams
  3. Whitepapers were boring while preparing for the exam
    • I could only glimpse first few pages of security whitepapers and nothing else
    • I got 1 or 2 questions from this white paper on "shared responsibility". It’s very important to give this specific paper a look
  4. AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Official Study Guide
    • I read the Summary and Exam Essentials for each topic. Glimpsed few topics in between
    • I got the eBook, I like to have an index to my left while I read. Sometimes I lose focus and having index helps me to estimate my next break and to focus on the current topic
  5. Last but not the least, amazing practice papers from Whizlabs
    • I purchased it for $19.95, it had 8 + 5 Full length practice tests
    • I scored 70+ in all the exams on first attempt, they give very good explanation which helps you think how to approach few of the questions.
    • I got around 5 to 7 questions for here
